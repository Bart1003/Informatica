How to Add Fish to a Garden Pond

Co-authored by wikiHow user: TheLegend27

Part 1: Preparing Your Pond

1. Check the dimensions of your pond.


In order to stock your garden pond with fish, you will need to make sure the pond is large enough to support the fish you intend to introduce. Fish need a certain amount of space, as well as variation in depth

  • Your pond should have a minimum 12-15 square feet of surface area in order to add fish.
