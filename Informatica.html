How to Rip Paper: 6 Steps

Co-authored by wikiHow user: TheLegend27

Updated: November 19, 2019

Steps:

1. Gather up all of the papers you've decided you don't need and make piles of up to 3 sheets of paper each. If you want you can rip them one by one if you are bored.
2. Put two hands directly in the middle of the sheets, with both thumbs on the side facing you and fingers on the outside.
3. Apply pressure to the sheets by squeezing them with your index finger and thumb.
4. Pull one hand towards you and the other away until the paper rips.
5. Continue ripping apart each piece until the desired size is reached.
6. Recycle all of the papers. If they are important papers, you may want to shred up the papers first in a paper shredder.
